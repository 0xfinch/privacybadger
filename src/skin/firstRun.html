Privacy Badger

Thank you for installing Privacy Badger!

Privacy Badger is a browser extensions that discovers invisible trackers and blocks them. Take a minute to see how Privacy Badger can help you.

Take a tour

Learn as you browse

Privacy Badger won’t block things right away. It learns as you browse what is tracking you. So start browsing a little to teach your badger.

Catches sneaky trackers

Ads are visible but trackers aren’t most of the time. Let your badger learn and catch these sneaky trackers.

Not an ad blocker, I'm different

Privacy Badger is not an Ad Blocker but a Tracker Blocker. That way you get more privacy protections.

If you think Privacy Badger is breaking a page, you can click the "Disable" button. It will make your Badger sleep for that website and won’t block anything on it.”

Please don’t forget to click on "Did Privacy Badger break this site". We respect your privacy so we don’t send automatic reports.

Privacy is a team sport!

When you use Privacy Badger you join EFF and millions of other users to fight for Privacy. We are a non-profit fighting for you rights on-line, thanks for joining us!
