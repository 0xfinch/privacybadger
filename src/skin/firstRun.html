Privacy Badger

Thank you for installing Privacy Badger!

Privacy Badger is a browser extension that discovers invisible trackers and blocks them. Take a minute to see how.

Take a tour

Learns as you browse

Is your Badger not blocking anything yet? Keep browsing! Instead of keeping lists of what to block, Privacy Badger is smart and automatically discovers trackers as you browse the Web. So start browsing a little to teach your Badger.

Catches sneaky trackers

Invisible tracking happens in all sorts of ways; ads are just the visible tip of the iceberg. Privacy Badger sends a signal to all third-party trackers telling them not to track you. If they ignore your wishes, your badger will learn to block them—whether they are advertisers or trackers of other kinds.

Not an ad blocker, I'm different

Privacy Badger starts blocking once it sees that tracker on three different websites. Three strikes and it's out! (That's how we determine a tracker is "tracking" you.) If you use other privacy tools or ad blockers, great! Privacy Badger will learn much less, but that's OK, it will catch whatever your other blockers miss.

If you think Privacy Badger is breaking a page, you can click the 'Disable' button. It will make your Badger sleep for that website and won't block anything on it.

Please don't forget to click on 'Did Privacy Badger break this site'. We respect your privacy so we don't send automatic reports.

Your Badger learns all by itself. Privacy badger will NEVER share data about what sites you visit unless you choose to report an error.

Privacy is a team sport!

When you use Privacy Badger you join EFF and millions of other users to fight for privacy. We are a non-profit fighting for your rights on-line, thanks for joining us!
